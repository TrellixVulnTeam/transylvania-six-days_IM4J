Event Rules

Regulations and compliances for the event!

Age limits information

1. No one under the age of 14 may race

2. To qualify to race we prefer you have done a race at least 10 miles in length in the past and you have pre-riden the course and provided proof.

3. Racers between 14 -16 must submit a resume and proof of successful pre-ride

ALL JR racers MUST submit a signed waiver from a parent no less than 2 weeks prior to race day.

General start info

All racers under 18 must have a chaperon on site watching out for their racing progress.Ages are determined by the racers age on race day.

Starting times will be staggered. See schedule. We use a gun start, and if you jump the gun you can be dnf’d or penalized the difference + 2 min. Jumping the gun is cheating

General event rules

1. Be responsible for your belongings and don’t throw trash on the trail.

2. Be nice to your fellow racers, always announce when you are passing and make sure they have a safe place to pull over, especially in the technical parts.

3. No short-cutting the course

4. Remember the desert is a fragile place, try to only pass when there is room, try to wait for a wide spot in the trail and try not to create unnecessary widening of our single track.

5. If you need to stop and take a break make sure you are well off course in a hardened area.
